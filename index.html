Common questions

What to know before you join

We want you to have clear expectations. This is a place to learn and improve, not a shortcut to easy money.

Is this financial advice?
No. Nothing in the community is financial, investment, or trading advice. We show you how we think, how we trade, and the rules we use. You are responsible for your own decisions and outcomes.
Do you guarantee results or profits?
No. Trading involves substantial risk and there is no guarantee of profit. Some members may lose money, especially while learning. You should never trade money you cannot afford to lose.
What skill level is this for?
We have members from “just starting out” to experienced traders looking to sharpen their process. Education content assumes you are willing to do the work and ask questions when something is unclear.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. You can cancel your membership at any time through the platform you signed up on. You will keep access until the end of your billing period.

Still not sure if it is the right fit? Join, explore the education and live channels, and ask questions. If the environment or structure is not what you need, you can cancel before the next billing cycle.
