FAQ

Common questions before joining.

Is this alerts or education?
This is an education-focused trading community. You will see trade ideas and examples, but the intent is to teach you how to build and execute your own plan — not to blindly copy someone else’s alerts.
What skill level is this for?
We have members who are just getting consistent with the basics and traders with more experience who want structure and community. As long as you respect risk and want to improve, you will fit in.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. All memberships are handled through Whop. You can cancel from your Whop dashboard at any time; your access continues until the end of your paid period.
Do you guarantee results or profits?
No. Trading involves substantial risk and there is no guarantee of profit. Some members will do well, others may not — especially while learning. You should never trade money you cannot afford to lose.
